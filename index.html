Happy & excited about Bangalore Marathi

"Spreading The Vivacious Colors Of Marathi Heritage".

Celebrating the Marathi Culture in Bengaluru since 2012. A young and dynamic Maharashtrian Group living and breathing our homeland and its diversity.

What started out as a small Facebook Group now having graduated to a full fledged community icon in Bengaluru. Get togethers, events and much more, the list is endless. Bengaluru Marathi has become a well known entity and a source of identity for all Maharashtrians staying here. Bengaluru Marathi believes in innovative ideas. Which also lead us to staring two very constructive groups. BM Path Finders, a group that helps all community people with skill enhancement and job search. BM Kannada a group dedicated to valuing the local culture and language.
